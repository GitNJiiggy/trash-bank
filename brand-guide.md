# Trash Bank Brand Guide

## Mission Statement

Clean communities, built from the ground up. Trash Bank turns litter into social capital — rewarding people for taking care of their neighborhoods while creating pathways to economic inclusion.

## Tone of Voice

- **Encouraging, not preachy** — We're celebrating people doing good, not shaming them into action
- **Witty, not cynical** — A touch of humor (like McClaw) but always optimistic
- **Inclusive, not patronizing** — Everyone from crypto-natives to homeless individuals can participate
- **Direct, not complex** — Clear calls to action, simple explanations

## Naming Conventions

| Term | Usage | Why |
|------|-------|-----|
| **Trash Bank** | Platform name | Familiar, trustworthy, clear purpose |
| **Trash Coins** | Database points | Accessible, not intimidating |
| **Trash Tokens (TTKN)** | On-chain currency | Standard naming for crypto users |
| **Garbage Cans** | Karma badges | Playful, memorable, earned-not-bought |
| **Trash Collectors** | Workers/Participants | Respectful, active role |
| **Trash Masters** | DAO leaders | Playful authority, earned title |

## Visual Identity

### Primary Colors
- **Charcoal Void** — `#0a0a0a` — Background, depth
- **Neon Green** — `#00ff88` — Accent, growth, positivity
- **White** — `#ffffff` — Text, clarity

### Secondary Colors
- **Cyan Teal** — `#00cccc` — Water, cleanliness
- **Warm Orange** — `#ffaa00` — Energy, action

### Typography
- **Headlines:** System font stack (SF Pro, Inter, Segoe UI)
- **Body:** Same stack, lighter weights
- **Monospace:** For token addresses, IDs

### Logo Concepts

1. **Overflowing Can**: Trash can with sparkles/stars rising — symbolizes transformation
2. **Coin in Can**: A coin being dropped into a bin — clean, direct
3. **Raccoon Mascot**: Friendly, helpful character in green vest — approachable, memorable

## Mascot: "Dumpy"

**Character:** A friendly raccoon in a green vest with a trash-can emblem.

**Personality:**
- Helpful and encouraging
- A bit mischievous (raccoons are curious)
- Never dirty — always clean and presentable
- Holds a gold coin, not trash

**Usage:**
- Onboarding screens
- Error pages ("Dumpy needs you to fix something...")
- Social media presence
- Gamification (earn Dumpy badges)

**Taglines:**
- "Let's clean up together!"
- "Your trash is someone's treasure."
- "Earn while you clean."

## Competitive Positioning

| Competitor | Our Angle |
|------------|-----------|
| Traditional cleanup | We pay people directly. Cash rewards, not just virtue signaling. |
| Gig apps (TaskRabbit) | We're blockchain-native. On-chain escrow, transparent rewards. |
| Charity programs | We're for-profit with social impact. Sustainable, not donation-dependent. |
| Other crypto projects | We have real utility. Trash collection = real work = real value. |

## Partnerships to Pursue

1. **McClaw** — Core integration, task infrastructure
2. **Base** — L2 blockchain, low fees
3. **City governments** — Municipal cleanup contracts
4. **Vending machine operators** — Redemption network
5. **Homeless shelters** — Distribution points for Trash Bank cards
6. **Corporate sponsors** — Fund vending machines, earn CSR credits
