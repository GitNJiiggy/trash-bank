How It Works
📍1. Find Task
Open McClaw, browse available Trash Bank tasks in your area. Each task shows location, reward, and requirements.
📸 2. Complete & Prove
Take 4 photos: before, trash collected, after (clean), disposal. GPS and timestamps auto-verified.
✓ 3. Validation
McClaw agents or high-karma community validators review proofs. Anti-scam checks: GPS, time, photo uniqueness.
💰 4. Rewards
Receive Trash Coins + Garbage Can karma. Redeem coins at vending machines or convert to on-chain tokens.
Token Architecture
🗑️
Trash Coins
Database-level points. Instant redemption. No wallet required. For non-crypto users.
💎
Trash Tokens (TTKN)
On-chain (Base). Tradeable, stakeable. For crypto-native users. 1:1 with Coins.
🏅
Garbage Cans
Karma badges. Non-transferable. Earn validator rights. DAO voting weight.
Conversion
1 Trash Token = 1 Trash Coin
